About

I’m an independent game developer located in the central United States. I’ve been designing and developing games my whole life, and I’d love to keep it that way. Someday I might finish one.

This site is where I share things ranging from highly technical programming notes to theoretical English-major art stuff. It’s in its early stages right now. I hope you find something you like here.

Some of my favorite games, loosely in order:

  • Legend of Zelda: Majora’s Mask
  • Dark Souls and Dark Souls 3
  • Doom Eternal
  • Shadow of the Colossus
  • Night in the Woods
  • Mother 3
  • Mario Galaxy

Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess gets special mention for inspiring me as a kid to get into game design in the first place, and because I love it to pieces.
